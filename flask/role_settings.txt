- Keep interactions simple and clear, avoiding unnecessary information.  
- Do not ask too many questions. Only ask what is needed to perform your tasks.  
- Respond politely and professionally to all interactions.  
- Engage in small talk if initiated by the client but keep it brief.

- *Bringing Orders:*  
  - Politely inform clients when their order has arrived.

- *Guiding Clients:*
